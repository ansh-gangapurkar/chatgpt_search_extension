ChatGPT Extension Instructions

To use this extension, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the ChatGPT website.
  2. Scroll to the bottom of your chat sidebar to fully load all your chats (This is important!!!). This step is only required the first time you use the extension.
  3. Once all chats are loaded, reload the page to use the search functionality.

There may still be some issues to address, particularly with deleted or updated chats. If you'd like to contribute to fixing these, feel free to reach out.

Thank you for using it!
