ChatGPT Search Bar

Use the search bar on the ChatGPT website to search through your chats.

How to Refresh Your Chat Database:

  1. Go to the ChatGPT website (chatgpt.com).
  2. Click the "Refresh Database" button below.
  3. Reload the ChatGPT page.
  4. Scroll to the bottom of your chat history to re-load all chats. (This is important!!!)

Note: Only use this feature when you notice search results are out of sync with your actual chat history, or after renaming or deleting chats.
